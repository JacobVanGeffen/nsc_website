About Us

The Catalyst committee is responsible for publishing and maintaining the Natural Sciences Council newspaper, Catalyst, and disseminating news, entertainment, and information throughout the College of Natural Sciences. Popular articles have received thousands of views, while projects designed by students for students receive even more viewership. The committee is led by the Catalyst Chair, who also serves as the Editor-in-Chief. A Co-Editor and several Assistant Editors round out the editing staff. The staff writers, in addition to the editors, produce daily content applicable to the Natural Sciences student.

Ask Cat

Q. What’s Ask Cat? A: Natural Sciences Council (NSC) values your opinion and wants your feedback. Do you have questions for the faculty and administration or the student representatives of CNS? Utilize NSC’s members and connections to get answers for your questions and concerns about academics, student-life, and more, from complaints about free printing to queries about study lounges. Submit your question to Ask Cat!

Write for Us

Are you interested in getting published in a diverse, student-run publication? Are you doing exciting things in the lab, volunteering, or just daily life? Is there a topic that sparks your passion? Are you part of an organization with a great mission and want to reach out to the CNS? If you answered yes to any of these questions,  please submit ideas, questions, and articles to nsc.catalyst.chair@gmail.com and let us publish your story!
